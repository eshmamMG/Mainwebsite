About Mosharaf Group

About us

In the ever-evolving textile industry, there is a strong need for reliable and innovative textile manufacturers. This section will provide insight into a prominent textile manufacturing company and its commitment to excellence in the field. As a leading textile manufacturer, our company takes pride in its rich history and expertise in producing high-quality textiles. With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of skilled professionals, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking top-notch textiles.

At our core, we prioritize delivering products that meet the highest standards of quality, durability, and design. Our manufacturing processes adhere to strict quality control measures to ensure that every piece of fabric leaving our facilities is flawless.

Our commitment goes beyond producing exceptional textiles; we also prioritize sustainability in our operations. As an environmentally-conscious company, we strive to minimize any negative impact on the environment by utilizing eco-friendly production methods and materials whenever possible.

With years of experience in the industry, we have developed strong relationships with clients worldwide. We understand the unique requirements of different markets and are dedicated to providing tailored solutions that meet their specific needs.

As a leading textile manufacturer, our company has been at the forefront of the industry for many years. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the market.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced machinery enable us to produce a wide range of textiles that cater to diverse industries and applications. From fabrics for apparel and home furnishings to technical textiles used in automotive and industrial sectors, our products are known for their superior quality and durability.

With a dedicated team of skilled professionals, we strive to meet the ever-changing demands of our customers. We prioritize customer satisfaction by ensuring timely delivery, competitive pricing, and personalized service. At our textile manufacturing company, we understand the importance of sustainability in today's world. We have implemented eco-friendly practices throughout our production process, from sourcing raw materials responsibly to minimizing waste and conserving energy. Our commitment towards sustainability reflects in every aspect of our operations. Whether you are looking for high-quality fabrics or seeking reliable textile manufacturing services, we are here to fulfill your requirements. Thank you for considering us as your trusted partner in textiles.

MOSHARAF COMPOSITE TEXTILE MILLS LTD

Mosharaf Composite Textile Mills Ltd. is one of the largest concerns in Mosharaf Group. MCTML is one of the most modern highly equipped Spinning Mills of Bangladesh that produces superior quality 100% export oriented knitted & woven yarns. We produce Carded, Combed, Slub, Organic. BCI, PC, CVC including all types of mélange, modal, viscose, linen & other blended, vortex, siro, inject, snow, open end, recycled etc. yarns. MCTML procures premium quality raw materials which enables to produce a consistently superior quality of yarns.




M.N. DYEING, PRINTING & WASHING MILLS LTD.

M.N. Dyeing, Printing & Washing Mills Ltd. has a per day capacity of Twenty Five (25) Tons of Knitting. Thirty Five (35) Tons of Knit dyeing. Ten (10) Tons of Yarn dyeing, Four (4) Tons of Fiber dyeing facilities, Fifteen (15) Tons of Fabric printing Facilities and Fifty Thousand (50,000) Pieces of Garments Printing facilities. M.N. Dyeing, Printing & Washing Mills Ltd. has a vertical setup for complete process of fabric. Along with our Yarn manufacturing unit, we are capable of providing complete support from yarn to ready dyed and printed fabric for 100% Export oriented Garment industries.




MOSHARAF APPARELS STUDIO LIMITED

Mosharaf Apparels Studio Limited is the initiation of Mosharaf Group into the RMG sector of Bangladesh, the core competency of Mosharaf Apparels Studio Limited is the complete supply chain of Garment industry which can value add up to 90% of the production cost with backward linkage facility of Yarn spinning. Color Mélange, Fabric Manufacturing-Solid, Y/D & AOP, own Dyes & Chemicals.




ASUCHEM LIMITED

Asuchem Limited is an International Chemical trading house in Bangladesh. The Company is mostly involved in marketing and sales of various Textile Dyes and Auxiliaries through indent sales covering a wide range of Customers' needs. We represent and deliver "World Class" in Bangladesh from BOZZETTO-ASUTEX, Italy/Spain. Our Corporate Office is in Red Crescent Borak Tower, 37/3/A, Eskaton Garden, Level-7 (SW), 71-72, Old Elephant Road, Ramna, Dhaka-1000.




M/S. MOSHARAF & BROTHERS.

M/S. Mosharaf & Brothers is the oldest and one of the most important sister concerns of the Mosharaf Group and is located at our Corporate Office in Dhaka with Four (04) Regional Offices/Sales Centers in Chattogram, Narayanganj, Noapara/Jashore and Nagarbari/Pabna. M/S. Mosharaf & Brothers is an International Trading House dealing mainly with the trade of various types of food grains such as Wheat, Maize, Corn, etc. Coal and Fertilizer is also being imported every year and being distributed across the country through our vast distribution network and various sales centers.
